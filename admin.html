System Guide

Operational Manual

Approvals

Manage new player registrations. Approving a player sets their base stats to Tier 1 and calculates their initial ΛPI to 5.

Player DB

View, search, and edit all active players. Click 'Edit' to manually adjust raw points (PS, I, O, D, GK) up to a maximum of 200. Titles and Roles auto-update.

Match Logger

The core tool for admins during games. Select a player and efficiently log multiple actions at once (e.g. 2 Goals, 3 Saves). The system automatically calculates and updates their raw points.

Season Reset

Used only at the end of a season. It performs a 'Soft Reset', deducting 50 raw points from every stat of every player, mimicking skill degradation.
